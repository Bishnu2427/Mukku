The Pipeline

How It Works

Eight intelligent stages collaborate to transform your text idea into a complete, ready-to-publish video.

Stage 1–2

Script & Scene Writing

Mukku reads your prompt and crafts a structured multi-scene script complete with shot directions, dialogue, and mood cues — all optimised for your chosen platform's format and duration.

Powered by Mukku Script Intelligence
📄
script_draft.json
Stage 3–4

Image & Clip Generation

Each scene is rendered as a photorealistic image by Leonardo.ai, then brought to life as an animated video clip by Kling.ai — producing cinematic motion from a single description.

Powered by Mukku Image & Clip Engine
Stage 5–6

Voiceover & Music

A natural-sounding AI voiceover narrates the script in your selected language, while Suno AI composes an original, mood-matched soundtrack — both timed to the visuals automatically.

Powered by Mukku Voice & Music Engine
♪ Suno AI — Energetic, BPM 128
Stage 7–8

Final Assembly

MoviePy assembles all clips, overlays captions in the correct language, mixes audio tracks, and renders the final MP4 at your platform's optimal resolution and aspect ratio — ready to download and post.

Powered by Mukku Smart Assembly
