MUKKU AI STUDIO — CAPABILITIES
================================


WHAT IT DOES
------------
Mukku AI Studio turns a text prompt into a fully produced MP4 video — complete
with visuals, animation, voiceover, and background music — in a single click.
No editing skills required.


CORE CAPABILITIES
-----------------

Video Generation
  - Converts any text description into a complete video (30 seconds to 5 minutes)
  - 8-stage automated pipeline: script > scenes > images > clips > voice > music > final video
  - Outputs a downloadable MP4, ready to post

AI-Powered Script & Scene Writing
  - Understands your prompt and extracts topic, tone, and duration automatically
  - Writes a full narration script tailored to your content
  - Breaks the script into individual scenes with visual descriptions

Image Generation
  - Creates one high-quality image per scene using Leonardo.ai
  - Styles: Photorealistic, Cinematic, Documentary
  - Aspect ratios: 16:9 Landscape, 9:16 Vertical, 1:1 Square

Video Clip Animation
  - Animates each scene image into a short video clip
  - Powered by Kling.ai / Pollo.ai / Google Gemini Veo

Voiceover Synthesis
  - Generates narration audio for every scene
  - Supports English + 11 Indian languages:
    Hindi, Bengali, Telugu, Marathi, Tamil, Gujarati,
    Kannada, Malayalam, Punjabi, Odia, Assamese
  - Voice gender selection: Auto, Female, Male

AI Music Composition
  - Generates a background music track via Suno AI, matched to the video's tone
  - Can be toggled on or off

Video Assembly
  - Merges all clips, voiceovers, and music into a single polished MP4
  - Uses FFmpeg for professional-grade encoding

USER MEDIA UPLOAD
-----------------
Users can personalise their video by uploading their own files in the composer:

  - Photos (JPG, PNG, WebP) — replace AI-generated scene images
  - Videos (MP4, WebM, MOV) — replace AI-animated clips
  - Up to 10 files, 50 MB each
  - Remaining scenes (beyond uploads) are still generated by AI
